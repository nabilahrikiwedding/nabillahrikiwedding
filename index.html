We Love Each Other

Our Story

  • For us, it happened quickly. We know marriage is a huge commitment and something you will have to work at for the rest of your life because it will not always be happy smiles. Some days it will be hard, very trying, and your faith will be tested..

  • We didn’t listen to the marriage experts who declared we were much too young. We ignored their advice to “find yourself” and build a stable life before entering a permanent relationship.

  • So call us crazy for getting married young if you want. You wouldn’t be the first, and you won’t be the last. But I wouldn’t change it for the world. We were young and naive, and we probably still are. Compared to many, we’re still that starry-eyed kid who doesn’t have a clue.

  • But we had the essentials—kind hearts deeply committed to each other. We barely developed our own personalities, let alone understood each other’s. Instead we focused on creating a foundation of trust and selflessness. We determined to carve our way through life together, no matter what obstacles came our way. We were impressionable, but we viewed that as a strength. We shaped and molded our personalities to complement each other and grew in love as we faced challenges hand-in-hand.

  • We’re oblivious to all the future curveballs life will throw, and we’re not sure how we’ll get through it. But we’re determined to do it together.
